THE BOY WHO CRIES WOLF

Once upon a time, there was a shepherd boy who watched over his flock of sheep on a hillside. One day, feeling bored, he decided to play a prank on the villagers below. He shouted, "Wolf! Wolf! The wolf is attacking the sheep!" The villagers, alarmed, rushed to his aid, only to find that there was no wolf.
The boy laughed at the villagers. A few days later, he played the same trick again. This time, the villagers were a bit skeptical but still came to help. Again, they found no wolf.
Then, one day, a real wolf came. The boy cried out for help, "Wolf! Wolf! Please, help!" But this time, the villagers, remembering the boy's previous false alarms, ignored him. The wolf attacked the flock, and the boy learned his lesson the hard way: dishonesty has consequences, and no one believes a liar, even when they tell the truth.

MORAL:- LIARS ARE NOT BELIEVED EVEN WHEN THEY SPEAK TRUTH
