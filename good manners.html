GOOD MANNERS

Brush your teeth twice a day.
Wash your hands before and
after eating.
Say 'PLEASE' when you are
politely asking someone to
do something.
Say 'SORRY' to apologize
for your mistakes.
Say 'THANK YOU' to express
gratitude.
Knock on the door before
entering a room.
Respect and obey elders.
Put away smartphones while
at dining table.
