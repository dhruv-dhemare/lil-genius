THE HONEST WOODCUTTER

Once upon a time, there was a woodcutter who lived in a small village. He worked hard every day to cut wood from the forest and sell it in the market to support his family. One day, while cutting wood near a river, the woodcutter's axe slipped from his hand and fell into the river. He was very upset because that was his only means of earning money.
Suddenly, a fairy appeared from the river holding a golden axe. She asked the woodcutter if it was his axe. The woodcutter replied, "No, mine was just a simple iron axe." Then with a silver axe, he still replied the same.
Impressed by his honesty, the fairy gave him all three axes - the golden one, a silver one, and his own iron axe. The woodcutter was overjoyed and thanked the fairy for her kindness.From that day on, the woodcutter became prosperous and never had to worry about money again.

MORAL:- HONESTY IS THE BEST POLICY
