CHATRAPATI SHIVAJI MAHARAJ

1st Chhatrapati of the Maratha Empire.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a 17th-century Indian warrior king who founded the Maratha Empire in western India. Known for his military genius, he resisted Mughal expansion and established a sovereign Hindu state. His legacy includes innovative warfare tactics, progressive governance, and a symbol of Maratha pride. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was born in 1630 in the hill fort of Shivneri in Maharashtra, India. His birthplace was significant, as it provided a strong foundation for his upbringing in a region marked by rugged terrain and a rich cultural heritage. From this humble beginning, Shivaji would rise to become one of the most revered figures in Indian history
