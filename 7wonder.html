7 WONDERS OF THE WORLD

GREAT WALL OF CHINA-(CHINA)

The great wall of china is present in China. It was built in 7th century BC.The first emperor Qin Shi Huang was the one to build it. It is 21196 km in length.It is a series of fortifications that were built across the historical northern borders of ancient Chinese states. It served the purpose of protection against various nomadic groups from the Eurasian Steppe.

TAJ MAHAL-(INDIA)

The Taj Mahal was built by a famous Mughal emperor named Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Building of the Taj Mahal began in the year 1632. It took about 20 years to complete! The Taj Mahal is made of beautiful white marble It's about 73 meters tall (240 feet) and the main dome is 35 meters (115 feet) tall. It has a big pool in front called the "reflecting pool."

COLOSSUM -(ROME)

The Colosseum, also known as the Flavian Amphitheater, was built by the ancient Romans. It was constructed during the Roman Empire, around 70-80 AD. The Colosseum is a huge amphitheater, which means it's like a giant stadium. It was used for all sorts of events, like gladiator fights, animal hunts, and even mock sea battles! The Colosseum is made of stone and concrete, and It's about 50 meters tall (160 feet) and could hold around 50,000 spectators. The Colosseum is a symbol of ancient Rome's power and engineering skill

PETRA-(JORDON)

It is originally known to its inhabitants as Raqmu or Raqēmō. It is a historic and archaeological city in southern Jordan. It is famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system. Petra is also called the "Rose City" because of the colour of the sandstone. It built in early 5th century BC.

MACHU PICCHU -(PERU)

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Eastern Cordillera of southern Peru. It is a 2,430-meter (7,970 ft) mountain ridge.It is often referred to as the "Lost City of the Incas". It is the most familiar icon of the Inca Empire. It is located in the Machupicchu District within Urubamba Province above the Sacred Valley

CHRIST THE REDEMEER-(RIO DE JENERIO)

It is an art deco statue of Jesus Christ. It is located in Rio de Janeiro in Brazil. It was constructed in between 1922-1931. The statue is 98 ft in height. It was designed by Paul Landowski This statue is the largest Art Deco style sculpture in the world. A symbol of Christianity around the world, the statue has also become a cultural icon of both Rio de Janeiro and Brazil.

CHICHEN ITZA-(MEXICO)

Chichen Itza is an ancient city built by the Mayan civilization. It's located in the Yucatan Peninsula of Mexico. The Maya people built Chichen Itza over a thousand years ago, between the 7th and 10th centuries AD. One of the most famous structures in Chichen Itza is the Pyramid of Kukulcan, also known as El Castillo. It's a huge step pyramid with four sides, each with 91 steps. That's a total of 364 steps, plus the top platform makes 365, one for each day of the year!
