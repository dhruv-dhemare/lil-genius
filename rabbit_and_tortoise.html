RABBIT AND THE TORTOISE

Rabbit and Tortoise once decide to race to a flagpole. Rabbit was trying to show tortoise that he is slow. They starts running towards the flagpole. Rabbit goes to ahead in the race.
Rabbit reaches near the finish line. Tortoise crawls at its usual pace. He thinks Tortoise won,t be able to catch up and decides to sleep for a while. While the rabbit is sleeping Rabbit continues with the slow speed.
While Rabbit is asleep the Tortoise slowly reaches the end,and wins the race. While the Rabbit is still fast asleep.

MORAL:- SLOW AND STEADY WINS THE RACE
