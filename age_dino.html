AGE OF DINPSAURS

This period, known as the Mesozoic Era, lasted from about 252 to 66 million years ago.

It's divided into three periods: the Triassic, Jurassic, and Cretaceous.

Dinosaurs dominated the land, sea, and sky during this time, and the Earth's continents were configured differently than they are today.
