ICE AGE

Earth has experienced several ice ages throughout its history, but the most recent one, often referred to as the Pleistocene Epoch, occurred from about 2.6 million to 11,700 years ago.

During this time, large parts of the Earth's surface were covered by glaciers, and many species adapted to cold climates, including mammoths and saber-toothed cats.
